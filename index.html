Smart Rockets

Simulates natural selection in a population of "rockets" whose survival and reproduction depend on reaching a target while avoiding obstacles. Each rocket starts at the bottom and has 400 frames to navigate using random motion vectors ("DNA"). The closer a rocket gets to the target, the higher its chance to reproduce by mixing DNA with another successful rocket, with a small chance of mutation.
